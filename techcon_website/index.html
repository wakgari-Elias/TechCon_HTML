What is TechCon?

TechCon Training and Expo has a trusted history of providing industry experts to lead discussions on the technical development and solutions that improve key high voltage maintenance programs and asset management strategies for the aging electrical grid infrastructure. Since 1996, TechCon has been produced by TJ|H2b Analytical Services, a senior leader in the oil analysis industry.

When is TechCon?

February 14-16, 2024 at the Hyatt Regency, Sacramento, CA.
Arrive on Tuesday, February 13, 2024 for our “Badges & Beer” regi-ception 5:00 PM – 6:30 PM. Gain access to PG&E staff members as well as other west coast utilities and vendors.

Who is TechCon for?

TechCon is for anyone who is involved in the maintenance of electrical equipment.

What is the cost of TechCon?

The cost of TechCon is $1,000.00 per person.

Why Attend TechCon?

If you want to learn about industry changes, upcoming technology, and share recommendations or ideas, attend TechCon. Hear details about sensitive topics, topical subjects and if you want access to high level utility industry experts who are eager to share their vast knowledge and experience, TechCon® is the educational event for you.

Who Should Attend?

Delegates—whether a newcomer or a seasoned professional—will include generation, transmission and distribution executives and personnel; electrical engineers; maintenance and asset managers; security experts, technology directors, consultants; service providers; manufacturers; insurance companies; utility maintenance specialists; and technician and substation superintendents.
